# Intro.
In ultrasonic medical imaging, the original signals are composed of individual RF (radio frequency) signals. 
During this process, we need to progressively transform these signals into the final ultrasound images through various processing methods.

These steps can be roughly categorized as follows:

Frequency filtering
Signal demodulation
Signal enveloping
Logarithmic compression
Image processing

The following example will use the raw signals obtained from the Terson Model 3000 system and its Model 5C2A curved probe as the initial processing data.
